स्वाइन फ्लू क्या

स्वाइन फ्लू क्या और क्यों

स्वाइन फ्लू श्वसन तंत्र से जुड़ी बीमारी है।यह वायरस एच1 एन1 के नाम से जाना जाता है। मौसमी फ्लू में भी यह वायरस सक्रिय होता है।

डा.संजीव कपूर के अनुसार 2009 में जो स्वाइन फ्लू हुआ था, उसके मुकाबले इस बार का स्वाइन फ्लू कम पावरफुल है, हालांकि उसके वायरस ने इस बार स्ट्रेन बदल लिया है। जो लोग सूअर पालन के व्यवसाय में है और लम्बे समय तक सुअरों से संपर्क में रहते है उनको स्वाइन फ्लू होने का जोखिम अधिक रहता है| 2009 में नो इसका टीका भारत में उपलब्ध नहीं था पर अब इसके टीके का इस्तेमाल सफलतम  रूप से किया जा रहा है और इस की मदद से वर्तमान में एच 1 एन 1 फ्लू से बचाव संभव है

कारण

यह एक बेहद संक्रामक रोग है और इसका प्रसारण एक व्यक्ति से दुसरे व्यक्ति तक बेहद जल्द फैलता है| जब कोई स्वाइन फ्ल से प्रभावित व्यक्ति खांसता है या छिकता है तो मुह और नाक से निकले वाली छोटी बूंदों से साथ इसके वायरस भी बाहर आ जाते है और हवा में और कठोर सतह पर अगले 24 घंटो तह जाते है| हवा में रहने वाली बेहद छोटी बुँदे किसी भी व्यक्ति सो सांस के द्वारा अन्दर जाकर संक्रमित कर सकती हैं| संक्रमित व्यक्ति से किसी भी प्रकार से संपर्क में आने से दूसरा व्यक्ति भी स्वाइन फ्ल से संक्रमित हो सकता है|

सर्दी के दिनों में स्वाइन फ्ल के मरीजों की संख्या बढ़ जाती है|

The H1N1 form of swine flu is one of the descendants of the strain that caused the 1918 flu pandemic. As well as persisting in pigs, the descendants of the 1918 virus have also circulated in humans through the 20th century, contributing to the normal seasonal epidemics of influenza. However, direct transmission from pigs to humans is rare, with only 12 recorded cases in the U.S. since 2005. Nevertheless, the retention of influenza strains in pigs after these strains have disappeared from the human population might make pigs a reservoir where influenza viruses could persist, later emerging to reinfect humans once human immunity to these strains has waned. Swine flu has been reported numerous times as a zoonosis in humans, usually with limited distribution, rarely with a widespread distribution. Outbreaks in swine are common and cause sig -nificant economic losses in industry, primarily by causing stunting and extended time to market. For example, this disease costs the British meat industry about £65 million every year

सामान्य फ्लू और स्वाइन फ्लू के लक्षण एक जैसे ही होते हैं, लेकिन स्वाइन फ्लू में यह देखा जाता है कि जुकाम बहुत तेज होता है। नाक ज्यादा बहती है।

 

Symptoms
Common symptoms in most of the swine flu cases.
In case of swine flu the real problem is that the symptoms can be same as seasonal flu. They usually include-
i) cough        
ii) fever
iii) sore throat
iv) stuffy or runny nose
v) body aches
vi) headache
vii) chills
viii) fatigue
In worst cases symptoms are extreme body aches, sinus pressure with crushing headaches, deep coughing, runny nose and no appetite. Patient is hungry, but doesn't find anything worth eating, and when he/she eats everything just tastes horrible.
Just like seasonal flu, swine flu can prompt more problems, including pneumonia and respiratory failure. Furthermore it can make conditions like diabetes or asthma more terrible. In the event of problems like shortness of breath, extreme regurgitating, stomach agony, dizziness, or confusion, call a doctor or go to emergency immediately.
Please keep in mind that swine flu is a deadly disease and should be taken very seriously as it can be deadly.

स्वाइन फ्लू का इलाज

स्वाइन फ्लू होने के पहले 48 घंटों के भीतर इलाज शुरू हो जाना चाहिए। पांच दिन का इलाज होता है, जिसमें मरीज को टेमीफ्लू (Tamiflu- Oseltamivir) रोगी की उम्र व् वजन के हिसाब से  दी जाती है। 

आयुर्वेद  के मुताबिक आयुर्वेद में स्वाइन फ्लू को वात कफज ज्वर के नाम से जाना जाता है जो कि वात (हवा) और कफज (पानी) के बिगड़ने से होता है। इसका वायरस सांस के जरिए बॉडी में प्रवेश करता है और कफ, सर्दी, जुकाम, बॉडी पेन, फीवर जैसे सिम्प्टम पैदा करता है। डॉक्टर मुल्तानी का कहना है कि बचाव ही सबसे बेहतर उपाय है। अगर कुछ सावधानियां बरती जाएं और कुछ आयुर्वेदिक उपायों पर अमल किया जाए तो स्वाइन फ्लू के खतरे से बचा जा सकता है। डॉ. मुल्तानी बता रहे हैं कुछ ऐसे ही घरेलू और आयुर्वेदिक उपाय।

    Treatment for swine flu

If a person get's infected with the H1N1 virus(swine flu), antiviral drugs can help make the illness milder and make the patient feel a lot better faster. They may also prevent serious influenza problems.
For treatment, antiviral drugs work best if started within 48 hours of symptoms. Beside antivirals, supportive care at home or in a hospital focuses more on controlling fevers, relieving the pain caused by the flu and maintaining fluid balance, as well as identifying and treating any other infections or medical problems. According to the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, the use of oseltamivir (Tamiflu) or zanamivir (Relenza) for the treatment and/or prevention of infection with swine influenza viruses is recommended. However, the good news is that most of the people infected with the virus make a full recovery. The virus which was isolated in the 2009 outbreak of the virus is known to be resistant to amantadine and rimantadine.
In the U.S.. on AdnI 27. 2009. Relenza and Tamiflu antiviral drugs to treat the swine influenza virus were given emergency use authorizations by the FDA. The agency issued these EUAs to allow treatment of patients younger than the current age approval allows and to allow the widespread distribution of these drugs, including by volunteers.

    कब तक रहता है वायरस

    एच1एन1 वायरस स्टील, प्लास्टिक में 24 से 48 घंटे, कपड़े और पेपर में 8 से 12 घंटे, टिश्यू पेपर में 15 मिनट और हाथों में 30 मिनट तक एक्टिव रहते हैं. इन्हें खत्म करने के लिए डिटर्जेंट, एल्कॉहॉल, ब्लीच या साबुन का इस्तेमाल कर सकते हैं. किसी भी मरीज में बीमारी के लक्षण इन्फेक्शन के बाद 1 से 7 दिन में डिवेलप हो सकते हैं. लक्षण दिखने के 24 घंटे पहले और 8 दिन बाद तक किसी और में वायरस के ट्रांसमिशन का खतरा रहता है.

    Precautions

    Following precautions to be kept in mind to stay away from swine flu.
    Stay Home- If you are sick or have any flu­like symptoms, don't go out of your home! not even to class or job. Swine flu symptoms have a tendency to mimic symptoms of regular flu and cold symptoms. Wash hands frequently- Keep Washing hands to avoid swine flu. Anything you touch may be infected, so keeping hands clean will help you stay away from the virus.
    Contain your cough- When coughing or sneezing contain it in the crook (inside elbow) of your arm. This will prevent you from transferring germs to your hands and then to any object you touch. Swine flu is highly infectious, and can spread easily by touching an infected object and then touching your face. A flu mask is highly advisable to help you avoid infection in enclosed spaces.
    Hand Sanitizer- Try keeping a hand sanitizer with you in your pocket or purse all the time. If you have things that others have touched and are handing over to you, use your hand sanitizer properly to avoid swine flu infection. In addition, as a major precaution avoid shaking hands or other hand to hand contact whenever possible. Also try avoiding kissing on the cheek or other face to face contact when greeting someone.
    Avoid crowded areas- In a country or an area with several cases of swine flu, stay out of crowds as much as possible. Close proximity to other people will increase your chances of to getting swine flu. A surgical mask is a must in areas of high traffic concentration. In areas with H1N1 outbreak try to stay at least 3-6 feet away from people.
    Public Facilities- Touch public handles and other's pens or other writing instruments as little as possible. These are loaded with germs that may carry the H1N1 virus.
    Eating or drinking- Do not even think of eating or drinking after others without ensuring com -plete sanitization of containers or utensils you're having food in. Swine flu can be transmitted by contact with infected eating utensils as well.
    If you come in contact with the virus go to the doctor within 36 hours, and ask for Tamiflu or Relenza. These antiviral medicines will halt the progression of H1N1 virus in your body.

    Go to the Doctor- Rush to a doctor immediately if you develop any symptoms of swine flu(or even a seasonal flu). Swine flu can be life threatening, and it is really important to get to a physician immediately if you think you have swine flu symptoms. Both Tamiflu and Relenza are antiviral medicines that are currently effective against some strains of Swine Flu. These medicines have to be taken within 36 hours of flu infection for full effectiveness.
    In addtion, when you are travelling by air or in a train, keep in mind all of the precautions stated above as there is a high chance of coming in contact with the H1N1 virus because of being in an enclosed space with so many people.
